zais financial corp . announces operational update related to flooding in louisiana 
red bank , n . j . , aug . {num} {num} / prnewswire / -- zais financial corp . ( nyse : zfc ) ( " zais financial " or the " company " ) announced today that its mortgage banking subsidiary , gmfs , headquartered in baton rouge , la . , was executing its business continuity plan in light of the severe flooding which has occurred in southern louisiana . in particular , gmfs remains fully operational ; its systems fully functional and employees are reporting to work . gmfs continues to close and sell loans , although at a reduced volume . while the situation continues to evolve and gmfs is not yet in a position to quantify the impact of the flooding on its current business and financial results , gmfs anticipates that it will experience higher delinquencies and a decrease in servicing income while its impacted customers recover . 
michael szymanski , zfc president , stated " our first concern is with the welfare of our employees and customers in the face of this natural disaster . gmfs is taking an active role in assisting those most affected by the flooding , while remaining open for business as our dedicated employees continue to report to work and focus on our recovery . we expect to release further information on the financial impact of this event on gmfs when we have completed our assessment over the coming weeks . fortunately , we do not expect the situation in southern louisiana to delay or otherwise affect our pending business combination with sutherland asset management corporation . "
