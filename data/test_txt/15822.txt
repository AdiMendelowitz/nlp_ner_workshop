article vii 
limitation of liability 
to the maximum extent that maryland law in effect from time to time permits limitation of the liability of directors and officers of a corporation , no present or former director or officer of the corporation shall be liable to the corporation or its stockholders for money damages . neither the amendment nor repeal of this article vii , nor the adoption or amendment of any other provision of the charter or bylaws inconsistent with this article vii , shall apply to or affect in any respect the applicability of the preceding sentence with respect to any act or failure to act which occurred prior to such amendment , repeal or adoption .
