{enum} severability . if any provision of this plan or any award agreement or any award ( a ) is or becomes or is deemed to be invalid , illegal or unenforceable in any jurisdiction , or as to any person or award , or ( b ) would disqualify this plan , any award agreement or any award under any law the administrator deems applicable , then such provision should be construed or deemed amended to conform to applicable laws , or if it cannot be so construed or deemed amended without , in the determination of the administrator , materially altering the intent of this plan , award agreement or award , then such provision should be stricken as to such jurisdiction , person or award , and the remainder of this plan , such award agreement and such award will remain in full force and effect .
