about stellar biotechnologies , inc . 
stellar biotechnologies , inc . ( nasdaq : sbot ) is the leader in sustainable manufacture of keyhole limpet hemocyanin ( klh ) , an important immune-stimulating protein used in wide-ranging therapeutic and diagnostic markets . klh is both an active pharmaceutical ingredient ( api ) in many new immunotherapies ( targeting cancer , immune disorders , alzheimer's and inflammatory diseases ) as well as a finished product for measuring immune status . stellar biotechnologies is unique in its proprietary methods , facilities , and klh technology . we are committed to meeting the growing demand for commercial-scale supplies of gmp grade klh , ensuring environmentally sound klh production , and developing klh-based active immunotherapies .
